Why a Website?

Technology Addiction, a real problem that effects most people without them even knowing. The problem stems from the instant gratification we get from various forms of socializing through technology.

The main culprit is social media. Social media has it pros, it lets people keep in touch and communicate in a safe way

The problem comes from the instant gratification we get from say a like or positive comment. It gives off positive chemical reactions in your brain. Now when it becomes a constant thing you can get addcited to that chemical response, so when you do not get the result you where hoping for(ex. not enough likes or negative comments) it can give a much more negative reaction in your brain, depraving you of those positive chemcial reaction.

This does not only apply to social media, it also applies to things such as games as well. A game in your hand that you can play whenever where ever you want. Then you can get addicted to it and when you get negative feedback or are not able to play the game you can get the same

All this can cause:

  • Addiction

  • Social isolation

  • Depression
