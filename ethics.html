Medical Field:

The medical field has always incorporated technology into their workplace. Some might say now they have gone too far. A common argument is the use of robotic equiment during surgery. Surgeons can perform their operations within the same hospital, or across the globe. At first glance it seems like a good idea due to rare cases can see the speciliast they need without having to travel or not being able to see them at all. The flip side is, should a patient trust the bond between surgeon and technology enough to put their life in their hands?

The medical field also has adapted new ways to perform simplier visits. Normal check ups can be done online through a video call. Minor medical problems, questions, etc can be done without stepping foot into the doctors office.
